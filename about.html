Beginning

In 2012, a brilliant idea sparked in the mind of Lea L. Fabular, to embark on a venture of homemade siomai. Originally sold at Capalonga Elementary School, the allure of Nanay Lea's siomai was warmly received, inspiring her to extend beyond the school and sharing it with the whole town. The beginning of this small family business was marked by the creation of carts that traversed the town. On school days, a delightful scene would unfold as children eagerly gathered around these carts, all craving just a bite of Siomai ni Nanay Lea.

Expansion

In 2018, Nanay Lea's youngest son, Noriel L. Fabular, took on a significant role in expanding the family business. He ventured to Daet and established the first-ever branch of Siomai Ni Nanay Lea. This bold step was aimed at exploring new horizons and seeking fresh opportunities beyond their hometown. The migration to Daet marked a pivotal moment for the family's siomai business, paving the way for growth and reaching out to a wider audience. With Daet as the new frontier, Siomai Ni Nanay Lea blossomed into a beloved local gem. Noriel's dedication and passion echoed the essence of his mother's legacy, captivating the taste buds of Daet's residents and visitors alike. The expansion not only brought their delicious siomai to a broader audience but also honored the tradition behind each flavorful bite. As word of mouth spread, the humble siomai business grew, weaving its own tale of success and community appreciation in the heart of Daet.

Franchise

In the wake of pandemic-induced closures in 2020, Siomai ni Nanay Lea faced financial strain but found a lifeline in 2021 by embracing resellers. This strategic shift extended their reach beyond local confines, empowering a network of entrepreneurs eager to partner with the brand. Engaging resellers not only diversified distribution channels but also served as a catalyst for reconnecting with communities, homes, and events, reigniting the love for Nanay Lea's flavorsome siomai. This reseller network acted as ambassadors, amplifying the brand's presence and fostering new connections, showcasing adaptability and resilience in turbulent times.
